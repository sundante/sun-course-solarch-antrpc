# 01 — Apic Calibration

**Overview:** Role, mission, culture, and interview loop. The module that decides whether you sound like someone who has internalized Apic — or someone who has read Apic's website.

---

## What This Module Tests

Two things, simultaneously:

1. **Whether you understand the role accurately** — Pre-Sales IC, India/APAC, "trusted technical advisor," eval-framework builder, voice-of-customer-to-Product. Most candidates misunderstand this as a generic Solutions Architect role; it isn't.
2. **Whether you've internalized Apic's mission as operating mode, not vocabulary** — the difference between *referencing* Constitutional AI and *operating* in a safety-first way. Apic interviewers spot performative mission alignment in 90 seconds.

This module is the foundation for every later module. Get it wrong and the rest of the prep is built on sand.

## Strong-Hire bar for Module 01

You can answer all three of these on the spot, in under 2 minutes each, without notes:

- Why Apic specifically — referencing actual Apic research, not generic AI passion
- Why this role at this stage of your career — owning the IC lateral move with confidence, not apology
- Why Claude is the right tool for *your customers* — meaning a real customer profile (probably BFSI/regulated), not a hypothetical one

If any of these three lands at Lean No today, do not apply. Iterate.
