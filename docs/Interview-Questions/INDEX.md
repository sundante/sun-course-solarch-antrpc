## Q&A categories (per module, per the meta-prompt)

Every module's bank is structured the same way:

- **5 recruiter-style questions** — high-level fit, motivation, background
- **5 hiring-manager questions** — past projects, judgment, customer instinct
- **5 technical architecture questions** — Claude-specific design questions
- **5 values/behavioral questions** — safety judgment, ambiguity, tough calls
- **3 executive-communication questions** — translate same architecture across audiences
- **3 CISO/security questions** — the trust-with-security-leadership conversation

**Total per module: ~26 questions.**
**Total across course: ~312 questions** by Week 6.

Each question has:
- The question
- Strong-Hire / Hire / Lean No / Strong No rubric
- A worked Strong Hire answer
- The most common Lean No mistake
